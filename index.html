Staff Roles

Each staff member has a designated role based on their skills and responsibilities:

  • Trial Moderator – New staff learning the basics of moderation.
  • Moderator – Actively enforces rules and handles player issues.
  • Senior Moderator – Leads moderation and mentors newer staff.
  • Admin – Manages staff actions, reviews bans, and approves disciplinary actions.
  • Event Planner – Organizes public and department events.

Staff Expectations

  • Remain active in both the game and Discord server.
  • Maintain professionalism in all interactions.
  • Do not abuse staff permissions or powers.
  • Use correct procedures when moderating or assisting members.
  • Report serious incidents to Management promptly.

Strike Policy

The strike system is used to hold staff accountable:

  • 1 Strike – Verbal/written warning.
  • 2 Strikes – Temporary suspension of staff duties.
  • 3 Strikes – Removal from the staff team.

All strikes are reviewed by Management or Executives and may be appealed via ticket.
