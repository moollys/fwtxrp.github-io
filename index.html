In-Game Regulations

1. Respect – Treat everyone with respect no matter their role or rank.

2. RDM – Do not kill anyone without proper RP reason.

3. VDM – Do not use your car as a weapon without a valid RP scenario.

4. FailRP – Keep actions realistic; no superhuman stunts or exploits.

5. NLR – After dying, forget the previous life. Don’t return to the scene.

6. Unauthorized Orgs – Don’t create unapproved gangs, groups, or departments.

7. Character Conduct – Act with realistic emotion and behavior at all times.

8. Driving – Obey traffic laws and drive realistically.

9. Banned Roleplays – No erotic RP, terror threats, suicide RP, or ToS violations.

10. Safezones – Spawns and Staff HQs are protected RP zones. Don’t attack there.

11. Common Sense – If it seems wrong, it probably is. Think before acting.

Discord Server Regulations

1. Respect – No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

2. Pinging – Only ping when necessary. Never ping upper management randomly.

3. Language – No excessive swearing or inappropriate content.

4. Content – Post in correct channels and stay on-topic.

5. Advertising – No self-promo or advertising other servers without approval.

6. Common Sense – Don’t try to game the rules. Be civil and constructive.

RTO Regulations

1. If topics or communication is not related to in-game events or callouts, you will be removed from the VC without warning. If you continue speaking off topic, moderation actions can be taken.

2. Remain respectful to all LEO within the RTO at all times, arguing or trying to speak louder than others will only lead to punishments.

3. Work together and communicate clearly and properly.
