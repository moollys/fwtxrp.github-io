Staff Overview

Welcome to the Fort Worth Texas Roleplay staff hub. This site provides essential info, tools, and expectations for team members. Use the tabs to navigate key areas including roles, onboarding, guidelines, and escalation paths.

Roles & Permissions

  • Trial Moderator: Entry-level staff, monitors basic VC & chat behavior.
  • Moderator: Handles reports, kicks, basic punishments, and daily moderation tasks.
  • Senior Moderator: Reviews appeals, trains trials, helps with advanced moderation cases.
  • Administrator: Manages staff coordination, leads team meetings, oversees moderation policies.
  • Management: Approves bans, handles department changes, addresses major community decisions.

Hiring Process

Our process for hiring new staff includes:

  • Application submission via Discord
  • Initial review by Senior Mod/Admin
  • Interview via VC
  • Trial period monitoring (2 weeks min.)
  • Performance review and promotion to Mod if successful

Staff Expectations

  • Maintain professionalism and maturity in all actions
  • Remain active in VC and patrols
  • Log all disciplinary actions properly
  • Treat all members fairly, without bias
  • Report major issues to higher staff or management

Contact Chain

If you're unsure who to contact, follow this chain of communication:

  1. Trial/Mod issues → Senior Moderator
  2. Disputes or advanced moderation → Administrator
  3. Major server concerns or bans → Management
