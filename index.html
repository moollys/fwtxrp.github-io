In-Game Regulations

  1. Random Deathmatch: No full-kills unless justified. Avoid excessive or mass killings. Killing FD is not allowed.
  2. Vehicle Deathmatch: No random collisions or unrealistic driving. Shooting at other cars without reason is prohibited.
  3. Cuff Rushing: Do not arrest or cuff others randomly. Use -cuffs- responsibly with proper justification.
  4. Scenarios: Do not interfere in roleplay scenes unless you are a participant. Avoid causing disruptions.
  5. Uniforms: Wear only approved or whitelisted uniforms as per your role.
  6. Guns: Use only allowed or role-specific firearms. Choose options suited for the scenario.
  7. Liveries: Use department or whitelisted liveries that align with your role.
  8. Terms of Service: Follow Roblox's Terms of Service.
  9. Unrealistic Avatars: Avoid avatars that obstruct views, provide invisibility, or exploit glitches. Fur suits aren't allowed.
  10. New Life Rule: Do not retain memories from a previous life. Avoid returning to prior scenes or roles.
  11. Fail Roleplay: Avoid unrealistic, disruptive, historically inaccurate, or animal roleplays.
  12. Drones: Drones are allowed but must not interfere with roleplay or interrupt scenes.
  13. Safe Zones: Civilian Spawn, Police Department, Sheriff's Department, Department of Transportation, Fire Department.
  14. Booster Perks: Using perks without @Server Boosters status will result in removal.
  15. Grapplers: Avoid using grapplers, unless you are a whitelisted department member. Failure to abide by this will result in a kick.
  16. No Intention to Roleplay: Avoid standing on top of vehicles, shooting while driving, ignoring all roleplay, etc.
  17. GTA Driving: No reckless driving, such as exceeding speed limits, driving against traffic, or improper use of roadways, is permitted.
  18. VC Rules: It is mandatory to be in a roleplay voice channel while participating in roleplay scenarios.

Banned Guns & Vehicles

The following items are banned from all in-game use to maintain balance and realism:

  • Guns: Rocket Launchers, Flamethrowers, Mini-guns
  • Vehicles: Military Tanks, Armed Helicopters, Invisibility Vehicles
  • Use of banned items during roleplay will result in immediate disciplinary action.

Department Regulations

Each department must adhere to the following rules to ensure proper roleplay standards:

  • Departments must conduct roleplay professionally and realistically.
  • All members should wear approved department uniforms and follow chain-of-command protocols.
  • Use of department vehicles and equipment is restricted to roleplay purposes only.
  • Departments should regularly update their SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) in coordination with administration.

Mafia Regulations

  • Mafia roleplay must stay within agreed boundaries and avoid disrupting overall server experience.
  • Respect law enforcement roleplay; avoid unrealistic or excessive use of force.
  • All major mafia actions must be approved by the event staff to prevent conflicts.
  • Use of weapons and vehicles must comply with banned items list and server guidelines.

Voice Chat Regulations

  • Keep voice communication clear and respectful at all times.
  • Do not use voice changers or disruptive sound effects.
  • Use push-to-talk when possible to reduce background noise.
  • Voice chat should be used to enhance roleplay, not for casual or disruptive chatting.
