Chain of Command

Staff Regulation and Conduct

Board of Directors - Directors should not be DMed or pinged for questions, direct all inquiries to a ticket or staff chat. They oversee the entire server and make voted decisions based off what they think is best for the server. Affiliations and merges are oversee

Management Team - Management should never be directly contacted, they handle large reports or issues within IA or lower. They monitor performance and behavior of all staff, ensuring a consistency of rule enforcement across all levels.

Internal Affairs Team - Handles tickets, staff infractions, issuing strikes and reports. Bridge rank between management+ and lower ranks. Always go to them, and they will answer your question or direct you to us.

Administrative Team - Overlook moderators, permissions to hand out and manage bans. Fourth in command if in-game issues arise.

Moderation Team -Issues warnings and kicks in-game. Ensure rules are applied fairly and consistently, main in-game moderators.
