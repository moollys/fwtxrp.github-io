Chain of Command

Board of Directors - Directors should not be DMed or pinged for questions, direct all inquiries to a ticket or staff chat. They oversee the entire server and make voted decisions based off what they think is best for the server. Affiliations and merges are oversee

Management Team - Management should never be directly contacted, they handle large reports or issues within IA or lower. They monitor performance and behavior of all staff, ensuring a consistency of rule enforcement across all levels.

Internal Affairs Team - Handles tickets, staff infractions, issuing strikes and reports. Bridge rank between management+ and lower ranks. Always go to them, and they will answer your question or direct you to us.

Administrative Team - Overlook moderators, permissions to hand out and manage bans. Fourth in command if in-game issues arise.

Moderation Team -Issues warnings and kicks in-game. Ensure rules are applied fairly and consistently, main in-game moderators.

Staff Regulation and Conduct

  1. Staff are not permitted to perform law enforcement duties while on duty. This includes speeding, running red lights, and running stop signs.
  2. Roleplaying of any sort while on duty is prohibited and will result in punishment.
  3. If a user asks to heal, always check for roleplay context first, to avoid ruining roleplays.
  4. Rank Uniforms: Staff members must wear their designated rank uniforms while on duty to ensure they are easily identifiable.
  5. If you are wearing the staff uniform, nothing should be covering it, so that community members can identify your rank.
  6. Staff Cars Only: Use only the designated staff cars while on duty. Personal or roleplay vehicles are not allowed for staff use during shifts.
  7. Professionalism: Maintain a professional and respectful demeanor with players and fellow staff members at all times.
  8. Impartiality: Enforce rules fairly and without bias. Treat all players equally, regardless of their rank or status on the server.
  9. You must use 4+ letters at all times for all commands.
  10. Chain of Command: Follow the chain of command by pinging the lowest rank online that can assist you. (Mod → Admin → IA → Mgmt → BoD)
  11. Communication: Keep open lines of communication with other staff members. Report any serious issues or incidents promptly.
  12. In any mod and all mod calls, the user MUST have video evidence of any incidents happening. We do NOT take witnesses or any form of logs as proof.
  13. Avoid arguing with members; you have the final say, and incidents can be reported through a ticket.
  14. If a user asks for a supervisor or high rank, finish the mod scene, then guide them to our communication server to open a ticket.
  15. Bringing/Pinging HR+: You may NOT bring or ping HRs, nor teleport (TP) to HR without permission. Doing so will result in punishment. Feel free to PM them instead.
  16. If you are unresponsive for more than 5 minutes in-game, it will be considered shift grinding, resulting in punishment.
  17. GTA driving is only considered when the user is recklessly driving, such as driving constantly in opposite lanes, crashing into light poles, or driving along medians.
  18. You should never be parked at any medians or islands; continuous patrolling ensures proper role plays around the server.
