In-Game Regulations

Roblox Terms of Service

To roleplay here, you must adhere to Roblox's Terms of Service. Violating these terms may result in removal from the server.

1. Respect

Ensure you maintain a fundamental level of respect for your peers within the server. Regardless of your position, respect your peers at all times.

2. Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Random Deathmatch occurs when a player harms or kills others without justification. RDM disrupts realism and will result in warnings or disciplinary action.

3. Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

Vehicle Deathmatch occurs when a player damages or kills others using a vehicle without valid roleplay context. This includes reckless driving or crashing without a scenario.

4. Fail Roleplay (FRP)

FailRP happens when a player acts unrealistically, such as jumping off buildings unharmed or using exploits. You must act as you would in real life.

5. New Life Rule (NLR)

After dying, your character forgets everything from the previous life. Do not return to the same scene, take revenge, or retain memory of what happened.

6. Unauthorized Organizations

Creating or roleplaying as unauthorized mafias, departments, or military groups is prohibited. Doing so may result in a ban without warning.

7. Character Conduct

Your character must simulate real human behavior, including fear, pain, and emotional responses. This adds realism and improves server quality.

8. Vehicular Conduct

Drive realistically. Account for traffic, obey traffic laws, and adjust speed for weather or RP context. Reckless or GTA-style driving is not allowed.

9. Banned Roleplays

Roleplays that violate Roblox ToS are not allowed. This includes erotic RP, terrorism, suicide, or bomb threats. Keep roleplays safe and appropriate.

10. Safezones

Safezones are no-conflict areas such as spawns and Staff HQ. Roleplay can start there but cannot be forcefully initiated without RP context.

11. Common Sense

If it feels wrong or unrealistic, donâ€™t do it. Use common sense in situations not covered by specific rules. This is a realistic roleplay server.

Departments

Each department must adhere to the following rules to ensure proper roleplay standards:

  • Departments must conduct roleplay professionally and realistically.
  • All members should wear approved department uniforms and follow chain-of-command protocols.
  • Use of department vehicles and equipment is restricted to roleplay purposes only.
  • If you are not currently whitelisted for a department, you are only permitted to use "Standard Livery" labeled uniforms.
