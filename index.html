Welcome to Staff Hub

This portal contains everything FWTXRP staff need—roles, rules, tools, expectations, and escalation flow. Be sure to read carefully, check resources often, and stay up to date.

Staff Roles

  • Trial Moderator – Starter rank to learn moderation basics.
  • Moderator – Handles rule enforcement, reports, and moderation tickets.
  • Senior Moderator – Reviews punishment logs, handles appeals, and trains Trials.
  • Administrator – Oversees staff operations, escalated tickets, and event moderation.
  • Management – Final authority on bans, policies, and leadership.

Staff Expectations

  • Remain professional in all interactions—on duty or off duty.
  • Be active in patrols, VC moderation, and Discord involvement.
  • Do not abuse permissions—logs are monitored regularly.
  • Resolve issues with members respectfully and quickly.
  • Communicate clearly with your team and higher-ups.

Hiring & Promotion

How we bring in and evaluate new staff:

  • Submit an application through the Discord Staff Ticket System.
  • Attend a voice interview with Senior+ staff.
  • Go through a 2-week trial phase monitored by Seniors.
  • Receive promotion or dismissal based on logs and reports.

Escalation & Contacts

Unsure who to contact? Follow this chain:

  1. Trial → Ask your assigned mentor or Senior Moderator
  2. Mod → Contact Admin or escalate to Management
  3. All staff → Use tickets or DM Management for emergencies
